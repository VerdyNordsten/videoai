Which platforms do you support?

We support all major short-form video platforms including TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels, and more. You can upload videos directly or paste links from these platforms.

Absolutely! Pro and Agency users can export their AI-generated insights to Notion, Google Docs, CSV files, or integrate directly with their favorite tools via our API. This makes it easy to incorporate insights into your existing workflow.

Our platform is optimized for short-form content (15 seconds to 3 minutes). This includes TikToks, Reels, and Shorts. For longer content, we recommend breaking it into shorter segments for analysis.
